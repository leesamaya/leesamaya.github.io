Tutorial For A Normal Girl: How To Ask A Boy Out

Are you a nice, normal girl? Are you failing to summon the innocent boy you are madly in love with? Are you wondering what you are doing wrong, in this big, mad, serious world? Worry no more. Get your dream boy with these 5 simple steps.

Trois Gymnopedies (First Movement) - Gary Numan
  1. Find your victim
    Did you find him? Ok, good. Next:
  2. Look at them
    Where is he? Lost him in the crowd again? Oh! There he is! Ah, he’s so very pretty.
  3. FUCK! He caught me staring!
    This is where you are tested. You must fight every single atom in your body screaming at you to break eye contact. I urge you to hold it. There is a 67% chance his tummy will flip (depending on how good you look at the given moment). The other 33% is for the nonchalant men. We do not want them.
  4. Disappear mysteriously
    It’s a hard pill to swallow, but sometimes, you are better as a concept.
  5. Just kidding.
    Walk up to him and say this (word for word):
    Hello. My name is ________ (*your name*). I’m going to skip rocks by the river tonight. Do you wanna come?
    Now. There is a 67% chance he will say yes. The boy may look puzzled at first, and this is normal. You will witness the internal battle flash across his face, one between his heart and his brain. It sounds something like this:
    *I don’t think this is a good idea - But Brain, she’s so cute and funny and smart and sexy, how could I miss this? - She could murder you - And?*
    And so on. He’ll say yes. How could he not? You’re a catch. The Romantic era never really ended - the 18th century spirit of romance and sublime is still alive and well in our collective psyche. We search for meaning beyond logic. Sometimes that means spending a night skipping rocks by the river with a very nice, normal girl.
