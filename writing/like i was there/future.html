The Slow Cancellation of the Future

"I have a feeling – there’s not much evidence for this yet – that there will be major cultural re-awakenings occurring. Partly because time has been freed up for people. As people wake from the tyranny and dream of neoliberalism, starting to see that it’s never going to work, then they have to come up with alternative strategies: cultural strategies as well as political strategies. I feel that after five years since writing the book, the time is right for that kind of re-awakening to occur. But that, of course, may be just an optimistic disposition." — Mark Fisher, interview with Mustarinda (2013)
