Uccidere in silenzio 3
Lost Bag Poem
The train exhales me
in a huff of bodies that spill out its doors

I climb the stairs in twos
reach for my bag
but I only grasp air

Shit!
Lost bag
I run
but the train outruns me
So I stop
I weep

I am a child again
I must be supervised
Tie a string around my wrist
yank me back

Go away! Go away!
I say to the normal adult who comes near
You mustn’t love me!

I lose things
miss things
and
I’m embarrassed because
I’m not a real person yet

I perform this plea dramatically
for no one in particular

And in this fantasy
Mr. No One doesn’t move at all
He only stands and
looks at me and says
Hurry up!
Let’s find that bag.
