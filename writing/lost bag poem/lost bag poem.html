Uccidere in silenzio 3
Lost Bag Poem
The train exhales me
a huff of bodies spill out the doors

I climb the stairs in twos
reach for my bag
but grasp only air

Shit!
Check body check
Lost bag
I run, but I'm too late
So I stop


I am a child again.
I must be supervised
Tie a string around my wrist
yank me back
from my next mishap

Go away! Go away!
I say to the normal adult who comes near
You mustn’t love me

I lose things
and miss things
and
I’m embarrassed because
I’m not a real person yet

I perform this plea dramatically
for no one in particular

And in this fantasy
Mr. No One doesn’t move at all
He only stands
looks at me
and says
C’mon!
Let’s find that bag.
