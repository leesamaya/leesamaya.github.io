Uccidere in silenzio 3
Lost Bag Poem
The train exhales me
in a huff of bodies

I climb the stairs in twos
reach for my bag -
gone!

I run, but the train is indifferent
already elsewhere

I am a child again
I must be supervised
A string, my wrist, a sharp tug

A normal adult hovers
Their presense an intrusion
Go away, I say
You mustn’t love me

I lose things
miss things
and
I’m embarrassed because
I’m not a real person yet

I perform this plea dramatically
for no one in particular

And in this fantasy
Mr. No One doesn’t move at all
He only stands and
looks at me and says
Hurry up!
Let’s find that bag

Monday 2nd December after missing my flight home
