How To Ask a Boy Out: A Guide For The Ordinary Girl

You have identified a boy. He is ordinary, yet to you, he gleams. You hesitate, suspended in the pull of wanting and waiting. There are methods for this. They are straightforward and absurdly practical.

Trois Gymnopedies (First Movement) - Gary Numan
  1. Locate Him
    The boy exists somewhere near. Fix your gaze on him. He may not know yet he is the object of such focus.
  2. Observe
    He moves, as boys do, without knowing he is seen. You look, and then you are caught. Do not flinch. Resist the instinct to turn away. The world, in that moment, may shift ever so slightly in your favour.
  3. Accept The Odds
    Know that most will not reciprocate. The gaze is rare and its meanings rarer still. It is not a failing. Do not linger over this.
  4. Approach
    Walk up to him. Stand as if you belong where you are. Speak plainly:

  5. Wait, But Not For Him

    If he agrees, let it happen as it will. If he declines, go to the river anyway. The rocks wait. The river is yours. You are no less for trying. The world does not care for longing, but it sometimes bends to those who move through it as if it were bendable. That you dared is enough.
