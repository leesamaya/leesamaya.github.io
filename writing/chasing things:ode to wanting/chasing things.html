Chasing Things

An ode to wanting

We are creatures of want, are we not? These songs are restless like you and me. Some of you pretend not to want, but I know you want. Sometimes, want is not obvious. You can find it panicked and directionless, burning inward, while you wait for the kettle to boil. Or when you spend hours staring at trees like they've just appeared, their green overwhelming. Want persists. These songs don't solve it, but they get it. Press play.
